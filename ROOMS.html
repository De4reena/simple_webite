Our Rooms

The Royals Luxury and Liberty Hotel offers a variety of rooms to choose from, including standard rooms, suites, and penthouse suites. All of our rooms are equipped with the following amenities:

  • King-size bed
  • Private bathroom
  • Flat-screen TV
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Coffee maker
  • Mini-bar

Our suites and penthouse suites offer additional amenities, such as a separate living area, a fireplace, and a balcony.

Standard Rooms

Our standard rooms are perfect for guests who are looking for a comfortable and affordable place to stay. These rooms feature a king-size bed, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, a coffee maker, and a mini-bar.
